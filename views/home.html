How it works

How to get started in 5 simple steps

1.

Imagine you're hitting 10 shots to a target

You're hitting from the a certain point to the same target. It could be displayed like in this picture. The target doesn't necessarily have to be the pin, as long as the target is determined prior to the shot.

2.

How it would look like from an aerial view

These shots that you've just been hitting, would look like this from a bird's perspective.

3.

Imagine putting these shots into certain squares

We can take those shots and put them in specific boxes. Based on your target, we can classify them by how far away the shots are from the target.

4.

Then plot into this program

Based on how many shots there are in that specific box, we could plug in the frequency of these numbers into our program. For optimal experience, computers, but it's compatible with phones as well.

5.

Based on your given data, our algorithm calculates and finds your shot pattern

Based on these calculations, you can more easily see the pattern of your shots, and can see the probability of these numbers.
